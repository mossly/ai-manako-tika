An Act to authorise the repayment of certain loans to the Government of the Cook Islands,
and to authorise payments under a contract for the supply of an automatic telephone
exchange.
BE IT ENACTED by the Parliament of the Cook Islands in Session assembled, and by
the authority of the same, as follows:
1 Short title
This Act may be cited as the Finance (Asian Development Bank and T elephone
Exchange) Act 1982.
2 Asian Development Bank loan
There may from time to time, without further appropriation than this section, be
paid out of the Cook Islands Government Account to the Cook Islands
Development Bank any sums received by the Government of the Cook Islands
pursuant to a certain loan agreement made between the Asian Development
Bank and the Government of the Cook Islands, and signed at Manila on 30 April
1982.
3 T elephone exchange
There must be paid from time to time out of the Cook Islands Government
Account, without further appropriation than this section, such sums as may be
requiredâ€”
(a) to meet all payments due under a certain agreement between the
Government of the Cook Islands and Sumitomo Corporation and signed
at T okyo by the Postmaster General on 10 February 1982; and
(b) to meet all payments due, whether by way of principal, interest or
otherwise, under a certain agreement made between the Government of
the Cook Islands and the Republic of Nauru Finance Corporation, and
signed at Rarotonga by the Minister of Finance on 22 April 1982.
This Act is administered by the Ministry of Finance and Economic Management.
Published under the authority of the Parliament of the Cook Islands.
