An Act to repeal the Rarotonga Island Council, the Rarotonga Local Government
Act 1997
BE IT ENACTED by the Parliament of the Cook Islands in Session assembled, and by
the authority of the same as follows:
1 Short title and commencement
(1) This Act may be cited as the Rarotonga Local Government (Repeal) Act 2007.
(2) This Act comes into force on 8 February 2008.
2 Repeal and revocation
The Acts and Regulations contained in the Schedule are repealed and revoked
respectively .
3 Financial
Despite anything contained in the Ministry of Finance and Economic
Development Act 1995–96, the Financial Secretary is authorised, for the
ﬁnancial year 2007–2008, to transfer the balance of Output 14.7 of the Ministry
of Internal Af fairs appropriation (Rarotonga Community Development and
Governance) for expenditure by that part of the Ministry of Internal Af fairs
concerned with the cleaning of roadsides, streams, and road drainage on
Rarotonga.
4 Consequential
Any reference to a V aka Council in any enactment is deemed to be a reference
to such person, group of persons or organisation as may be determined by
Cabinet from time to time.
