An Act to correct omissions in the reappointment of Maarametua T etava as Commissioner
of Police during the period 1 November 2016 to 17 March 2020.
The Parliament of the Cook Islands enacts as follows—
1 Title
This Act is the Police (V alidation of Reappointment) Act 2020.
2 Principal Act
In this Act, the Police Act 2012 is called the principal Act.
3 Commencement
This Act comes into force on assent by the Queen’s Representative.
4 Act binds the Crown
This Act binds the Crown.
5 V alidation of reappointment of Commissioner of Police
(1) This section applies to Maarametua T etava who, in the period beginning on
1 November 2016 and ending at the close of 17 March 2020, occupied the
position of Commissioner of Police.
(2) Maarametua T etava is declared to have been reappointed under section 9 of the
principal Act—
(a) on 1 November 2016 until the close of 31 October 2018; and
(b) on 1 November 2018 until the close of 17 March 2020.
(3) The reappointments made under subsection (2) override any other
reappointments or purported reappointments made during the period.
(4) Anything done by Maarametua T etava during the period 1 November 2016 to
the close of 17 March 2020 is declared to be, and to have always been, as valid
as it would have been if he had been validly reappointed to the position of
Commissioner of Police.
This Act is administered by the Cook Islands Police.
Published under the authority of the Parliament of the Cook Islands.
