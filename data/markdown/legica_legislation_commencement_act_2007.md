An Act to bring into ef fect certain enactments.
BE IT ENACTED by the Parliament of the Cook Islands in Session assembled, and by
the authority of the same as follows:
1 Short title
This Act may be cited as the Legislation Commencement Act 2007.
2 Date of entry into force of speciﬁed enactments
The enactments listed in the Schedule shall be deemed to have come into
force—
(a) in the case of Acts, 30 days after the date of assent; and
(b) in the case of Regulations, 30 days after the date of making;
notwithstanding that no Orders in Executive Council as to commencement were
made, and notwithstanding that no notiﬁcation of commencement was given in
the Gazette.
3 Electoral Act 1966
For the avoidance of doubt it is hereby declared and enacted that the Electoral
Act 1966 was repealed by the Electoral Act 2004.
4 Local Government Electoral Regulations
It is hereby declared and enacted that the Local Government Electoral
Regulations 1968 shall continue in force as if made under the Rarotonga Local
Government Act 1977.
