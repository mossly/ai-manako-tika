BE IT ENACTED by the Parliament of the Cook Islands in Session assembled, and by
the authority of the same, as follows:
1 Short title
This Act may be cited as the Appropriation (W estpac Loan) Act 1989.
2 Provision for giving ef fect to Westpac loan agreement
There shall be paid out of such account of the Public Account, as the Minister
responsible for Finance may direct, as Statutory Expenditure and without further
appropriation than this Act, all payments required to be paid from time to time
to W estpac Banking Corporation pursuant to a loan agreement made betweeen
the Government of the Cook Islands of the one part and W estpac Banking
Corporation of the other part, and dated 28 November 1988 and as amended
from time to time.
[Section 2: amended, on 26 March 1990, by section 2 of the Appropriation (W estpac Loan)
Amendment Act 1989–90 (1989–90 No 39).]
This Act is administered by the Ministry of Finance and Economic Management.
Published under the authority of the Parliament of the Cook Islands.
