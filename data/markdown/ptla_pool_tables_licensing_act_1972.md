An Act to provide for licensing and restricting the use of pool tables.
BE IT ENACTED by the Legislative Assembly in Session assembled, and by the
authority of the same, as follows:
1 Short title
This Act may be cited as the Pool T ables Licensing Act 1972.
2 Interpretation
In this Act, unless the context otherwise requires,—
Minister means the Minister responsible for ﬁnance
owner in relation to any pool table includes the owner, hirer, bailee, and any
person having possession or control of any pool table
pool table means a table used for playing the games of pool, snooker, billiards,
or any game of the like kind.
3 Licensing of pool tables
(1) It is unlawful for any person to use any pool table for playing the games of pool,
snooker or billiards, or any game of the like kind, in respect of which a charge
is made for the use thereof or to which the public has access, unless a licence
has been granted in respect of that pool table pursuant to this Act.
(2) Every application for a licence of a pool table must be made in writing in the
form or to the ef fect of the Form No 1 in the Schedule.
(3) Every licence of a pool table must be granted by the Minister in the form or to
the ef fect of the Form No 2 in the Schedule and subject to the provisions of this
Act may be granted subject to such terms and conditions as the Minister thinks
ﬁt.
(4) Every licence must be granted as from 1 September in each year and be valid for
a period of 1 year.
4 Matters to be considered in granting licences
In considering any application for a licence of a pool table the Minister must
have regard to—
(a) the number of pool tables considered desirable to be licensed in the area
or district; and
(b) the suitability of the premises or building in which the pool table is to be
used.
5 Fees
There is payable on the grant of any licence a fee of $50 in respect of each pool
table licensed in the licence.
6 Restriction of hours
It is unlawful for any person to use any pool table for playing the games of pool,
snooker, or billiards, or any game of the like kind, in respect of which a charge
is made for the use thereof or to which the public has access, or any Sunday ,
Good Friday , or Christmas Day , or before the hour of 12 noon or after the hour

7 Restriction on age
It is unlawful for any person under the age of 16 years to use any pool table for
playing the games of pool, snooker, or billiards or any game of the like kind, in
respect of which a charge is made for the use thereof or to which the public has
access.

8 Of fences
(1) The owner of any pool table who acts in contravention of any provision of this
Act or who permits or allows any other person to use the pool table in
contravention of any provision of this Act commits an of fence and is liable on
conviction to a ﬁne not exceeding $400.
(2) Any other person who acts in contravention of any provision of this Act
commits an of fence and on conviction is liable to a ﬁne not exceeding $50.
9 Revocation of licence
If the Minister is satisﬁed that any licence has been obtained by fraud or
misrepresentation he or she may , by notice in writing given at any time to the
person to whom the licence was granted revoke the licence without refunding
any fee or any part of any fee paid in respect of the licence.
