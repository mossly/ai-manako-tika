An Act to deﬁne the Aratapu, Rarotonga.
BE IT ENACTED by the Legislative Assembly of the Cook Islands in Session
assembled, and by the authority of the same, as follows:
1 Short title
This Act may be cited as the Deﬁnition of Aratapu, Rarotonga Act 1967.
2 Deﬁnition of Aratapu, Rarotonga
(1) The road delineated on the Survey plans deposited in the Department of Lands
and Survey at Rarotonga under Numbers 1, 2, 3, 4 and 5 is hereby deemed to be
the road referred to as the Aratapu or Beach Road round the Island of Rarotonga
in the Order of the Cook and Other Island Land Titles Court made at Rarotonga
on 7 September 1904.
(2) The survey plans deposited in the Department of Lands and Survey at
Rarotonga under Numbers 1, 2, 3, 4 and 5 are hereby deemed to be the survey
plans referred to in Gazette Notice No 162 in the Cook Islands Gazette of
3 January 1905 at page 238.
This Act is administered by Legislative Services.
Published under the authority of the Parliament of the Cook Islands.
