An Act to authorise the repaying of certain loans by the Cook Islands Development Bank
BE IT ENACTED the Parliament of the Cook Islands in Session assembled, and by the
authority of the same as follows:
1 Short title
This Act may be cited as the Finance (Asian Development Bank) Act 1991.
2 Asian Development Bank loan
(1) There may from time to time, without further appropriation than this section, be
paid out of the Cook Islands Government Accountâ€”
(a) to the Cook Islands Development Bank such sums of money received
from the Asian Development Bank and intended by a loan agreement to
be paid to the Cook Islands Development Bank for its use; and
(b) to the Asian Development Bank such sums of money to meet all
payments due, whether by way of principal, interest or otherwise, under
a loan agreement between the Government of the Cook Islands and the
Asian Development Bank.
(2) For the purposes of this Act loan agreement means a loan agreement between
the Government of the Cook Islands and Asian Development Bank providing
for the lending of money by the Asian Development Bank and includes any
subsidiary agreement between the Cook Islands Government, Asian
Development Bank and the Cook Islands Development Bank.
This Act is administered by the Ministry of Finance and Economic Management.
Published under the authority of the Parliament of the Cook Islands.
