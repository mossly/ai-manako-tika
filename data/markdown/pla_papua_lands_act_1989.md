An Act to ef fect an assignment of certain lands
1 Short title
This Act may be cited as the Papua Lands Act 1989.
2 Preamble
WHEREAS William John Wigmore was the holder of an estate in leasehold in
(inter alia) the land described in the schedule hereto under and by virtue of the
Deed of Lease described in the said schedule.
AND WHEREAS the said William John Wigmore died at Papeete, in T ahiti on
or about 20 January 1952 and probate of his will bearing date 23 February 1949
was granted to Ronald William Stephens of Auckland, machinist as executor by
the Supreme Court of New Zealand at Auckland on 10 March 1958 under
Number 2019/57.
AND WHEREAS in the said Will the said William John Wigmore devised all
his leasehold interest in land to his son Alfred Charles Wigmore of Rarotonga,
planter.
AND WHEREAS by Deed dated 15 April 1958 the said Ronald William
Stephens as executor of the Will of the said William John Wigmore assigned
certain leasehold interests of the said William John Wigmore in certain lands to
the said Alfred Charles Wigmore.
AND WHEREAS the said assignment omitted to include all of the interests in
land devised unto the said Alfred Charles Wigmore by the said will, and in
particular the interests described in the schedule hereto.
AND WHEREAS it is desired to rectify such omission by now assigning to
Alfred Charles Wigmore the said lands described in the schedule hereto.
3 Assignment
(1) There is hereby assigned without further assignment or conveyance than this
section, all the leasehold interest of William John Wigmore in the lands
described in the schedule to this Act, from the said William John Wigmore to his
son Alfred Charles Wigmore of Rarotonga, planter.
(2) The assignment ef fected by subsection (1) is deemed to have occurred as if in all
respects it had been contained in and ef fected by a certain Deed of Conveyance
dated 1 April 1959 between Ronald William Stephens (as executor of the estate
of William John Wigmore), and the said Alfred Charles Wigmore.
