An Act to prohibit the leasing of land on Rarotonga motus.
BE IT ENACTED by the Parliament of the Cook Islands in Session assembled, in
accordance with the provisions of subclause (1), Article 41 of the Constitution of the Cook
Islands, and by the authority of the same, as follows:
1 Short title and commencement
(1) This Act may be cited as the Rarotonga Motus Prohibition of Leases
Act 1981–82.
(2) This Act is deemed to have come into force on 10 July 1981.
2 Prohibition of leases
(1) Despite anything contained in the Cook Islands Act 1915 or in any other
enactment or in any order of any court, no person shall be capable of leasing any
land on any of the motus of Rarotonga set out in the Schedule.
(2) Nothing in this section af fects any interest acquired in any land prior to the
commencement of this Act, provided that any dealing in respect of any lease,
whether by transfer, assignment or otherwise, and whether occurring before or
after the coming into force of this Act is prohibited.
3 Article 40 excluded
Article 40 of the Constitution has no application with regard to the provisions of
this Act.
