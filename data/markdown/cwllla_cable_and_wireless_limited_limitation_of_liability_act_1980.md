An Act to limit the liability of Cable and Wireless Limited in certain matters arising out
of its operation.
BE IT ENACTED by the Legislative Assembly of the Cook Islands in Session
assembled, and by the authority of the same as follows:
1 Short title
This Act may be cited as the Cable and Wireless Limited (Limitation of
Liability) Act 1980.
2 Interpretation
In this Act, unless the context otherwise requires, telecommunication means
any communication by way of the transmission, emission or reception to, from
or within the Cook Islands of signs, signals, writing images, or intelligence of
any nature by wire, radio, optical or other electromagnetic systems.
3 Liability in tort
No proceedings in tort shall lie against Cable and Wireless Limited in respect of
any loss or damage suf fered by any person by reason ofâ€”
(a) failure to provide, or delay in providing, a telecommunication service,
apparatus associated therewith or a service ancillary thereto:
(b) failure, interruption, suspension or restriction of a telecommunication
service or a service ancillary thereto or delay of, or fault in,
communication by means of a telecommunication service:
(c) error in or omission from a directory for use in conjunction with a
telecommunication service.
4 Liability in contract
Proceedings in contract against Cable and Wireless Limited in respect of any
loss or damage suf fered by any person for any of the reasons stated in
section 3(b) shall be limited to the recovery by the person af fected of the fee or
charge made by Cable and Wireless Limited for the transmission of the
particular communication which was not transmitted or incorrectly transmitted
in breach of the contract for transmission.
5 Liability of of ficer or servant
No of f icer or servant of Cable and Wireless Limited shall be subject, except at
the suit of Cable and Wireless Limited to any civil liability for any loss, or
damage in the case of which liability of Cable and Wireless Limited is excluded
by section 3 or 4.
This Act is administered by Cable and Wireless Limited.
Published under the authority of the Parliament of the Cook Islands.
