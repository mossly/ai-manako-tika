An Act to prohibit leases of land on Aitutaki Motus.
BE IT ENACTED by the Legislative Assembly of the Cook Islands in Session
assembled, in accordance with the provisions of Article 41(1) of the Constitution of the
Cook Islands, and by the authority of the same, as follows:
1 Short title
This Act may be cited as the Aitutaki Motus Prohibition of Leases Act 1970.
2 Prohibition of leases
(1) Despite anything contained in the Cook Islands Act 1915 or any other enactment
or any order of any court, no person shall be capable of leasing any land on any
of the motus of Aitutaki set out in the Schedule to this Act.
(2) Nothing in this section af fects any interest acquired in any land prior to the
commencement of this Act.
3 Article 40 excluded
Article 40 of the Constitution has no application with regard to the provisions of
this Act.
