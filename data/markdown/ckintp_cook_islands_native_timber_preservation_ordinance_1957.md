An Ordinance made by the Resident Commissioner of Rarotonga with the advice and
consent of the Legislative Council of the Cook Islands for the preservation of native trees
by the control of their export.
1 Short Title
This Ordinance may be cited as the Cook Islands Native Timber Preservation
Ordinance 1957.
2 Interpretation
In this Ordinance, native timber means logs and sawn pieces of dressed and
undressed timber of trees indigenous to the Cook Islands.
