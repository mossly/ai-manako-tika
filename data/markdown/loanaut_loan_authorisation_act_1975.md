An Act to authorise loans from the Asian Development Bank for public work.
BE IT ENACTED by the Legislative Assembly of the Cook Islands in Session
assembled, and by the authority of the same, as follows:
1 Short title
This Act may be cited as the Loan Authorisation Act 1975.
2 Interpretation
In this Act, unless the context otherwise requires, public work means any work
of a public nature executed on behalf of the Crown and includes rendering any
services in connection therewith and any construction manufacture or industry
of beneÔ¨Åt to the national economy .
3 Authority to borrow and secure loan
The Minister responsible for Finance is hereby authorised to borrow such sums
as may be required from time to time from the Asian Development Bank, on the
security of and charged upon the public revenues of the Cook Islands for any
public work.
4 T erms and conditions of loan
All money borrowed under the authority of section 3 is borrowed under and
subject to the provisions of the Government Loans and Contracts Act 1968.
5 Repayment of loan
All money payable by way of interest or principal in repayment of the loan
authorised by section 3 is statutory expenditure and is hereby appropriated
accordingly .
This Act is administered by the Ministry of Finance and Economic Management.
Published under the authority of the Parliament of the Cook Islands.
